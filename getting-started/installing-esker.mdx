# 📥 Installing Esker

Esker is designed to work smoothly across different environments. Follow the guide below to install Esker on your preferred platform.

## 🖥️ **Installing Esker on Desktop**

Esker offers a **desktop application** for a seamless offline experience. 

### **Windows**
1. **Download the latest version** from the [Esker website](https://esker.app/download).
2. Open the downloaded `.exe` file.
3. Follow the installation prompts.
4. Launch Esker from the Start Menu or Desktop shortcut.

### **Mac**
1. **Download the Mac version** from the [Esker website](https://esker.app/download).
2. Open the downloaded `.dmg` file.
3. Drag Esker into the **Applications** folder.
4. Open Esker from **Launchpad** or **Finder > Applications**.

### **Linux** *(Coming Soon)*
We're working on a **Linux version**. Stay updated by joining our [Esker Community](https://discord.gg/eskerapp).

## 🌐 **Using Esker on the Web**

If you prefer not to install an app, you can use Esker directly in your browser:

1. Open [Esker Web](https://esker.app) in **Google Chrome, Edge, or Firefox**.
2. Sign in (if required).
3. Start writing immediately!

💡 **Tip:** The web version saves your progress locally, but we recommend **manually saving your work** regularly.

## 📱 **Mobile Version** *(Coming Soon)*

A **mobile-friendly** version of Esker is in development. Stay tuned for updates!

## 🔧 **Troubleshooting Installation Issues**

### **Windows Issues**
🔹 Esker won’t open? Try running it as an **Administrator**.  
🔹 Installation blocked? Disable **Windows Defender SmartScreen** temporarily.

### **Mac Issues**
🔹 **“Esker can’t be opened”** message? Go to **System Preferences > Security & Privacy > Open Anyway**.

### **General Issues**
If you're facing any issues, check our **[Troubleshooting Guide](troubleshooting/common-issues)** or reach out on **[Discord](https://discord.gg/eskerapp)**.

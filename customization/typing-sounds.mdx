# 🎶 Typing Sounds & Typewriter Mode  

Esker lets you **customize the sound of your keystrokes**, adding an immersive touch to your writing sessions. Whether you prefer the nostalgia of a mechanical typewriter or the modern feel of a mechanical keyboard, **typing sounds can help you stay in the flow**.

## 🔊 **Enabling Typing Sounds**  

Typing sounds add an **auditory feedback loop**, making writing feel **tactile and satisfying**.  

### **How to Enable Typing Sounds**  
1. Click **☰ Menu** → **Options** → **Typing Sounds**.  
2. Select a sound profile:  
   - 🎹 **None** – No sound (default).  
   - ⌨️ **Classic Typewriter** – A mechanical typewriter sound.  
   - 🖥️ **IBM Beam Spring** – A deep, tactile keyboard click.  
   - 🎵 **Modern Mechanical** – Clicky mechanical keyboard sounds.  
   - 💨 **Topre Soft Tactile** – Smooth, dampened keystrokes.  
   - 🔥 **Holy Panda** – Popular tactile switch clicks.  
3. Click **Apply** to enable the selected sound.  

💡 **Tip:** You can fine-tune the **volume** of typing sounds in the settings!  

## 📝 **Using Typewriter Mode**  

Typewriter Mode keeps the **current line of text centered**, mimicking the experience of a **classic typewriter**. This can help **reduce distractions and maintain writing flow**.  

### **How to Enable Typewriter Mode**  
1. Click **☰ Menu** → **Enable Typewriter Mode**.  
2. Alternatively, press **F9** (keyboard shortcut).  
3. The **active line** will stay **locked in the center of the screen** as you type.  

🔹 **Prevents excessive scrolling**, so your eyes stay focused.  
🔹 **Encourages continuous writing** without losing track of your position.  
🔹 **Pairs well with Dark Mode & Typing Sounds** for an immersive experience.  

## 🎯 **Best Practices for Maximum Focus**  

To create the ultimate distraction-free writing experience:  

✅ **Combine Typewriter Mode + Focus Mode** (press **F10**).  
✅ **Use Vintage Green or Dark Theme** to reduce screen glare.  
✅ **Enable typing sounds** to stay in rhythm with your writing.
